About Vertex Global Ltd

Headquartered in Los Angeles, California with European operations in Liverpool, United Kingdom, Vertex Global Ltd is a premier international trading company specializing in the import and export of premium coffee, fine wines, and personalized luxury tableware and glassware.

With deep expertise across global supply chains, we source directly from renowned producers and estates worldwide, ensuring authenticity, quality, and sustainability. Our bespoke customization services allow clients to create unique, personalized pieces for hospitality, retail, and private collections.

Premium Coffee Selection

Ethiopia

The birthplace of coffee. We source exceptional single-origin beans from the Yirgacheffe, Sidamo, and Guji regions. Known for their bright acidity, floral notes, and complex berry and citrus profiles. Many are naturally processed or washed, offering unique terroir-driven characteristics.

  • Yirgacheffe: Jasmine, bergamot, lemon zest
  • Sidamo: Blueberry, chocolate, wine-like finish
  • Guji: Tropical fruit, honey, silky body

Colombia

World-renowned for balanced, sweet coffees with medium acidity and full body. Our selections from Huila, Antioquia, and Nariño offer caramel sweetness, nutty undertones, and clean, lingering finishes. Many farms practice shade-grown and organic methods.

  • Huila: Brown sugar, red apple, creamy mouthfeel
  • Antioquia: Milk chocolate, almond, balanced acidity

Brazil

The world's largest coffee producer. We offer premium Brazilian coffees from Minas Gerais and São Paulo regions, featuring smooth, chocolatey profiles with low acidity and nutty sweetness. Ideal for espresso blends and everyday enjoyment.

  • Minas Gerais: Dark chocolate, hazelnut, full body
  • Santos: Mild, sweet, with toasted almond notes

Kenya

Iconic for its vibrant, wine-like acidity and intense fruit flavors. Our AA and AB grade coffees from the Central and Rift Valley regions deliver bold blackcurrant, tomato, and citrus notes with exceptional clarity and complexity.

We also source specialty coffees from Guatemala (Antigua), Costa Rica (Tarrazú), Indonesia (Sumatra Mandheling), and Jamaica (Blue Mountain) upon request. All beans are freshly roasted to order or supplied green for roasters.

Fine Wine Collection

France

Home to some of the world's most prestigious wines. We work with renowned estates in Bordeaux, Burgundy, Rhône Valley, and Champagne.

  • Bordeaux: Classified Growths from Médoc, Saint-Émilion, and Pomerol – rich, age-worthy reds
  • Burgundy: Premier and Grand Cru Pinot Noir & Chardonnay from Côte de Nuits and Côte de Beaune
  • Champagne: Prestigious houses and grower-producers offering elegant sparkling wines

Italy

From the rolling hills of Tuscany to the volcanic soils of Sicily, we offer exceptional Italian wines.

  • Tuscany: Brunello di Montalcino, Chianti Classico Riserva, Super Tuscans
  • Piedmont: Barolo and Barbaresco – powerful Nebbiolo-based reds
  • Veneto & Sicily: Amarone, Valpolicella, and elegant Nero d'Avola

Spain

Renowned for bold reds and world-class sparkling wines. We source from Rioja, Ribera del Duero, Priorat, and Penedès.

  • Rioja: Tempranillo Reserva and Gran Reserva with elegant oak integration
  • Ribera del Duero: Powerful, structured reds from Tinto Fino
  • Cava: Traditional method sparkling wines offering excellent value

New World Highlights

We also curate exceptional wines from:

  • Napa & Sonoma Valley (USA): Cabernet Sauvignon, Chardonnay, and cult wines
  • Margaret River & Barossa Valley (Australia): Bold Shiraz and elegant Cabernet
  • Central Otago & Marlborough (New Zealand): World-class Pinot Noir and Sauvignon Blanc
  • Mendoza (Argentina): Malbec with intense fruit and structure
